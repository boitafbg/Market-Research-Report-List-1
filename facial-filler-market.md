<p><p>The facial filler market consists of various types, each offering unique properties. Hyaluronic Acid (HA) fillers provide natural hydration and volume, making them popular for facial rejuvenation. Calcium Hydroxylapatite (CaHA) fillers stimulate collagen production, suitable for deeper wrinkles and facial contouring. Poly-L-lactic Acid (PLLA) fillers promote gradual collagen growth, ideal for treating volume loss over time. Poly-Methyl-Methacrylate (PMMA) fillers offer a semi-permanent solution, providing lasting support for facial structures. Each type caters to different aesthetic needs and patient preferences.</p></p>
