<p><p>The plastic bucket market mainly comprises HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) and PP (Polypropylene) types. HDPE buckets are known for their durability, chemical resistance, and UV stability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications and outdoor use. In contrast, PP buckets offer flexibility, lightweight properties, and good thermal resistance, making them suitable for household and food storage applications. Both materials provide a cost-effective solution for various industries, catering to consumer demands for practicality and sustainability in everyday items.</p></p>
