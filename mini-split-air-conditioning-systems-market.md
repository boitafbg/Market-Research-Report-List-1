<p><p>Mini split air conditioning systems are divided into two primary market types: air cooling systems and water cooling systems. Air cooling systems utilize refrigerant to absorb heat from indoor air, providing efficient and localized cooling without ductwork. In contrast, water cooling systems rely on water circulation to dissipate heat, requiring infrastructure for water supply and drainage. Both systems are used for residential and commercial applications, offering flexibility, energy efficiency, and the ability to control temperatures in individual zones.</p></p>
