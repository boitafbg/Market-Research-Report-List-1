<p><p>Piezoelectric biosensors are analytical devices that utilize the piezoelectric effect to detect biological analytes, such as proteins, DNA, and pathogens. These sensors convert mechanical stress from binding events into electrical signals, enabling real-time monitoring of biomolecular interactions.
