<p><p>The Teflon capacitor market is categorized into two main types: axial and radial Teflon capacitors. Axial Teflon capacitors feature leads extending from both ends, allowing for a compact design ideal for tight spaces and efficient heat dissipation. In contrast, radial Teflon capacitors have leads that extend from one side, simplifying installation in various configurations. Both types are valued for their excellent dielectric properties and stability under high frequencies and temperatures, making them suitable for demanding electronic applications.</p></p>
