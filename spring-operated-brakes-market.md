<p><p>The spring-operated brakes market features two main types: hydraulic spring-operated brakes and electromagnetic spring-operated brakes. Hydraulic spring-operated brakes utilize hydraulic pressure to engage or disengage the braking mechanism, providing precise control and strong performance in various applications. In contrast, electromagnetic spring-operated brakes employ electromagnetic forces to activate the braking system, offering rapid response and reliable operation in critical situations. Both types are widely used in industrial machinery, transportation, and safety systems, enhancing overall functionality and safety.</p></p>
