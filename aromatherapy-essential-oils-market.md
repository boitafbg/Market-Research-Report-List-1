<p><p>The aromatherapy essential oils market comprises three main types: single oils, blend oils, and carrier oils. Single oils are pure extracts from individual plants, showcasing distinct therapeutic properties. Blend oils combine multiple essential oils to create synergistic effects, enhancing their benefits. Carrier oils serve as bases that dilute essential oils for safe application on the skin, facilitating absorption without irritation. Each type plays a crucial role in aromatherapy, catering to various needs and preferences in wellness practices.</p></p>
