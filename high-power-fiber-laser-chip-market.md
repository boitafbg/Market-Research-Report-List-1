<p><ul><li>DFB Laser Chip</li><li>VCSEL Chip</li><li>EML Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Power Fiber Laser Chip market consists of three main types: DFB Laser Chips, VCSEL Chips, and EML Chips. DFB (Distributed Feedback) Laser Chips are designed for precise wavelength control and high stability, commonly used in telecommunications. VCSEL (Vertical-Cavity Surface-Emitting Laser) Chips are known for their efficient light emission and are used in data communication and sensing applications. EML (Electro-absorption Modulated Laser) Chips combine modulation and amplification capabilities, making them ideal for high-speed transmission in optical networks. Each type plays a specific role in advancing fiber laser technology.</p></p>
