<p><strong>The Military Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OEM</li><li>Aftermarket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military tires market consists of two main types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and aftermarket. OEM tires are specifically designed and manufactured for military vehicles by the original equipment producers, ensuring compatibility and optimal performance. These tires are typically sourced directly from manufacturers during vehicle production. In contrast, the aftermarket includes replacement tires not sourced from the original manufacturers. These tires are available for military vehicles in need of repairs or upgrades, often offering alternative options for performance and cost.</p></p>
