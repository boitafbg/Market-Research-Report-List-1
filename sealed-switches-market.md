<p><p>The sealed switches market includes several types: SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw), SPST-NC (Single Pole Single Throw - Normally Closed), and SPST-NO (Single Pole Single Throw - Normally Open). SPDT switches can connect one input to one of two outputs, making them versatile for various applications. SPST-NC switches are closed by default, allowing current flow unless activated, ideal for safety features. SPST-NO switches remain open until activated, commonly used for control functions. This diversity caters to distinct industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
