<p><ul><li>Singlemode OTDR</li><li>Multimode OTDR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Time-Domain Reflectometer (OTDR) market features two primary types: Singlemode and Multimode OTDRs. Singlemode OTDRs are designed for long-distance telecommunications networks, utilizing a single light path for minimal signal loss and high resolution. In contrast, Multimode OTDRs cater to shorter distances with multiple light paths, offering higher bandwidth and better performance in local area networks. Each type serves distinct applications, with Singlemode functioning optimally for expansive networks and Multimode being ideal for local connectivity needs.</p></p>
