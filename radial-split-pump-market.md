<p><p>The radial split pump market comprises three main types: single-stage, two-stage, and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed for applications requiring low to medium pressure with straightforward construction. Two-stage pumps feature two impellers for increased efficiency and higher pressure capabilities, suitable for moderate flow demands. Multi-stage pumps utilize multiple impellers to generate even greater pressure, making them ideal for high-rise applications and demanding industrial uses. Collectively, these pumps cater to various needs in fluid transfer and process applications.</p></p>
