<p><p>The hydrogenated DCPD resin market is categorized into two primary types: fully hydrogenated DCPD and partially hydrogenated DCPD. Fully hydrogenated DCPD is obtained through complete hydrogenation, resulting in a stable and high-performance resin suitable for applications requiring enhanced chemical resistance and thermal stability. In contrast, partially hydrogenated DCPD retains some unsaturation, offering unique properties that can be advantageous in specific formulations. Both types find applications in automotive, coatings, and adhesives due to their favorable mechanical and physical characteristics.</p></p>
