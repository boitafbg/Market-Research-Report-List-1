<p><ul><li>Hydrosurgical Debridement Devices</li><li>Mechanical Debridement Pads</li><li>Low Frequency Ultrasound Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wound debridement devices are essential in the management of chronic wounds by facilitating the removal of non-viable tissue. Hydrosurgical debridement devices utilize a concentrated stream of saline to dislodge necrotic tissue while preserving healthy skin. Mechanical debridement pads use physical force to exfoliate dead tissue, often in conjunction with suction. Low-frequency ultrasound devices employ sound waves to disrupt cellular structures, promoting tissue healing and accelerating the debridement process through non-invasive and precise action.</p></p>
