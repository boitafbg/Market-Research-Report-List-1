High frequency detectors, operating above 20 kHz, provide enhanced sensitivity to smaller ferrous and non-ferrous materials, making them ideal for applications requiring precision detection, such as in food safety and pharmaceuticals. Each type serves distinct market needs and applications.</p></p>
