<p><p>The Tour Guide System market is segmented into two primary types: Wired and Wireless Tour Guide Systems. Wired Tour Guide Systems utilize physical connections, often involving microphones, headsets, and cables, ensuring stable audio quality but limiting mobility. In contrast, Wireless Tour Guide Systems utilize wireless technology, allowing guides and participants to move freely while receiving clear audio communication. Both systems cater to different user needs, with wired options preferred for controlled environments and wireless systems favored for outdoor or dynamic settings.</p></p>
