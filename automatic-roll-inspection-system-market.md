<p><p>The Automatic Roll Inspection System market is segmented into three primary technologies: Eddy Current Technology, Ultrasonic Technology, and Others. Eddy Current Technology detects surface and subsurface defects by inducing currents in conductive materials, making it effective for non-destructive testing. Ultrasonic Technology employs high-frequency sound waves to identify internal flaws, ensuring material integrity. The "Others" category encompasses various alternative methods, such as radiographic and magnetic testing, providing additional options for quality assurance in manufacturing processes across industries.</p></p>
