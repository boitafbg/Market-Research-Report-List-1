<p><p>The Mullite Brick market comprises primarily two types: Sintered Mullite Brick and Fused Mullite Brick. Sintered Mullite Brick is produced through high-temperature processing of raw materials, offering excellent thermal stability and mechanical strength, making it suitable for high-temperature applications like kilns and furnaces. In contrast, Fused Mullite Brick is created by melting raw materials and cooling them rapidly, resulting in a denser and more durable product, ideal for industrial environments where thermal shock resistance is critical. Both types serve essential roles in various high-temperature industries.</p></p>
