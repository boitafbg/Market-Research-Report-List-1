<p><p>The pulverized coal burner market includes various types designed for efficient combustion of coal. The swirl type pulverized coal burner utilizes swirling airflow to enhance mixing and combustion efficiency, resulting in reduced emissions and improved thermal performance. In contrast, the once-through pulverized coal burner features a continuous flow of pulverized coal and air, promoting uniform combustion and optimal energy extraction. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for cleaner energy solutions in industrial and power generation applications.</p></p>
