<p><p>The LED inspection light market is categorized into magnetic and non-magnetic types. Non-magnetic LED inspection lights, on the other hand, do not have magnetic components and are designed for versatile use in various environments, such as construction sites or home projects, where attachment to metal surfaces isn't necessary. Both types offer enhanced visibility and functionality for inspection tasks.</p></p>
