<p><ul><li>Monocular Pupilometer</li><li>Binocular Pupilometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pupilometer market is categorized into two main types: monocular pupilometers and binocular pupilometers. Monocular pupilometers measure pupil size using one eye, making them suitable for quick assessments and portable use. In contrast, binocular pupilometers assess both eyes simultaneously, providing more comprehensive data on eye alignment and function, which is crucial in various clinical and research settings. Both types serve important roles in ophthalmology, optometry, and vision science, catering to different diagnostic needs and user preferences.</p></p>
