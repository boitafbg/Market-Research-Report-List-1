<p><p>Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) Boards and Sheets are engineered materials combining wood fibers and plastic, offering durability and aesthetic appeal. Both types contribute to sustainable building practices by utilizing recycled materials and reducing the need for solid wood.</p></p>
