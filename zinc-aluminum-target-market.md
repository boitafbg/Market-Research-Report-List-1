<p><p>The zinc-aluminum target market includes two primary types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat surfaces that facilitate consistent deposition of coatings in applications like sputtering, providing uniform material distribution. In contrast, rotating targets utilize a cylindrical or disc shape that spins during operation, enhancing efficiency and target life by evenly distributing wear and heat. Both types are essential in various industries, including electronics and automotive, for producing high-quality thin films and coatings.</p></p>
