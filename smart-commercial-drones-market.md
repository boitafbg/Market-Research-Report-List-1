<p><p>The smart commercial drones market is primarily segmented into two types: Fixed-Wing and Rotary-Blade drones. Fixed-Wing drones resemble traditional aircraft and are ideal for long-distance flights, offering greater energy efficiency and longer endurance, making them suitable for applications like agriculture and surveying. In contrast, Rotary-Blade drones, often known as multirotors, are versatile and excel in maneuverability, allowing for vertical takeoff and landing. They are commonly used in aerial photography, delivery services, and inspection tasks due to their agility and ease of operation.</p></p>
