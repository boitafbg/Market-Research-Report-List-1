<p><ul><li>Episcopic Type</li><li>Diascopic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial inverted microscope market comprises two primary types: episcopic and diascopic. Episcopic microscopes are designed for observing reflective surfaces, amplifying the light reflected from the sample, making them ideal for materials testing and semiconductor inspection. In contrast, diascopic microscopes utilize transmitted light, illuminating samples from below, which is suitable for examining transparent or semi-transparent materials. Each type serves specific industrial applications, enhancing the analysis and quality control processes in various sectors such as manufacturing and materials science.</p></p>
