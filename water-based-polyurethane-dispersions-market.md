<p><p>The water-based polyurethane dispersions (PUD) market comprises three main types. Two-component PUD involves mixing a catalyst with the resin before application, offering enhanced performance and durability. One-component PUD, in contrast, is ready-to-use and cures through moisture exposure, providing convenience and ease of application. Urethane-modified PUD combines polyurethane with other polymers to improve specific properties like flexibility and adhesion. Each type caters to diverse applications in coatings, adhesives, and sealants, highlighting their adaptability and environmental benefits.</p></p>
