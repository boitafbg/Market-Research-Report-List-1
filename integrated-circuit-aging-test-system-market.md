<p><ul><li>Static Testing</li><li>Dynamic Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Integrated Circuit Aging Test System market is categorized into Static Testing and Dynamic Testing. Static Testing evaluates circuits under fixed conditions to assess reliability and longevity, typically measuring parameters like leakage currents and thermal performance. Dynamic Testing, on the other hand, simulates real-world operating conditions, subjecting circuits to varying voltages and temperatures to analyze performance under stress. Both types aim to predict failure mechanisms and enhance the lifespan of integrated circuits by identifying vulnerabilities during different operational scenarios.</p></p>
