<p><p>The gravity retaining wall market includes three main types: oblique, pitching, and upright walls. Oblique walls are inclined structures designed to redirect soil pressure and provide stability. Pitching walls feature sloped surfaces that enhance erosion control and manage drainage effectively. Upright walls stand vertically, maximizing space use and offering robust structural support. Each type serves unique landscaping and engineering needs, ensuring stability and protection against soil movement in various construction applications.</p></p>
