<p><p>The retail software market comprises two primary types: on-cloud and on-premise solutions. On-cloud software is hosted on remote servers, allowing retailers to access applications via the internet. This model offers scalability, flexibility, and automatic updates, making it ideal for businesses seeking cost-effective solutions. In contrast, on-premise software is installed locally on a company's hardware, delivering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to diverse retail needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
