Silicone lenses offer flexibility and excellent optical quality. Hydrophilic acrylic, or hydrogel lenses, absorb water for a soft feel and enhanced comfort, while hydrophobic acrylic lenses repel water, providing excellent clarity and stability. Each material caters to specific patient needs and surgical techniques, driving their demand in the ophthalmic industry.</p></p>
