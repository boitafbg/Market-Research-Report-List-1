<p><p>The glass microfluidic chip market features several types designed for various applications. Straight channel chips facilitate streamlined fluid flow for straightforward mixing processes. Chamber chips enable the confinement of samples for reactions or analyses, enhancing control over experimental conditions. Droplet generator chips produce microdroplets for precise handling of small volumes, ideal for high-throughput screening. Meander chips incorporate serpentine pathways, allowing for increased surface interaction and complex flow patterns, suitable for advanced biochemical assays and applications in diagnostics and research.</p></p>
