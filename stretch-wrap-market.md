<p><p>The stretch wrap market includes **Manual Stretch Wrap** and **Machine Stretch Wrap**. Manual stretch wrap is hand-applied, ideal for smaller operations or irregular loads, offering flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Machine stretch wrap is used in high-volume settings, applied by automated equipment for consistent tension and efficiency, reducing labor costs and material waste. Both types secure and protect goods during transit, but manual wrap suits lighter, varied loads, while machine wrap excels in large-scale, uniform packaging needs, catering to different industrial demands.</p></p>
