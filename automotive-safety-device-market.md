<p><p>The automotive safety device market is categorized into two main types: active safety systems and passive safety systems. Active safety systems are designed to prevent accidents through technologies such as anti-lock braking systems, lane departure warnings, and adaptive cruise control, actively supporting driver decisions. In contrast, passive safety systems focus on protecting occupants during an accident, including airbags, seatbelts, and crumple zones. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle safety, reducing injuries, and improving overall road safety.</p></p>
