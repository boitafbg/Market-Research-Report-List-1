<p><p>The industrial induction motor market comprises mainly two types: squirrel-cage and slip-ring motors. Squirrel-cage motors, known for their simple design and high reliability, are widely used in various applications due to their efficiency and lower maintenance needs. In contrast, slip-ring motors are favored in applications requiring high starting torque and variable speed control, as they incorporate slip rings for improved performance. Both types cater to different operational demands across industries, reflecting their unique advantages and applications.</p></p>
