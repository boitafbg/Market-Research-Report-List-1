<p><p>The 2 Phase Stepper Motors market is primarily divided into two types: Bipolar and Unipolar. Bipolar stepper motors utilize two winding coils with contrasting polarities, allowing for higher torque and efficiency but requiring more complex control systems. In contrast, Unipolar stepper motors have coils that are split, enabling easier control and simpler drive circuits, making them popular for applications where precision is essential but torque demands are lower. Both types cater to diverse industries, including robotics and automation.</p></p>
