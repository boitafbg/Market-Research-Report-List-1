<p><ul><li>Monocular</li><li>Binocular</li><li>Trinocular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical microscope market is categorized into three main types: monocular, binocular, and trinocular microscopes. Monocular microscopes feature a single eyepiece, offering simplicity and portability, making them suitable for basic applications. Binocular microscopes have two eyepieces, providing enhanced depth perception and comfort, ideal for more detailed observations. Trinocular microscopes include an additional ocular for camera attachment, allowing for simultaneous viewing and imaging, making them popular in research and educational settings. Each type serves distinct user needs and application areas.</p></p>
