<p><ul><li>Wet Tile Cutter</li><li>Dry Tile Cutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tile cutting tools market is primarily divided into wet tile cutters and dry tile cutters. Wet tile cutters utilize water to cool the blade, reducing dust and providing a smoother cut, ideal for ceramics and porcelains. Conversely, dry tile cutters operate without water, making them more portable and convenient for quick jobs, but they generate more dust and heat. Both types cater to different user needs, with wet cutters favored for precision and dry cutters for efficiency in small projects.</p></p>
