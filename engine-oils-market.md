<p><ul><li>Single-grade</li><li>Multi-grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engine oil market is divided into single-grade and multi-grade types. Single-grade oils, like SAE 30 or 40, maintain viscosity under specific temperature ranges, ideal for consistent climates. Multi-grade oils, such as 10W-30 or 5W-40, adapt to varying temperatures, offering better performance in both cold starts and high heat. Single-grade oils are simpler and cost-effective, while multi-grade oils provide versatility and enhanced engine protection, making them more popular in modern vehicles and diverse weather conditions.</p></p>
