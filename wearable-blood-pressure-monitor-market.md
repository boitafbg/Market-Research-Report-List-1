<p><p>The wearable blood pressure monitor market is primarily segmented into three types: wrist, upper arm, and finger monitors. </p><p>Wrist blood pressure monitors are compact and portable, ideal for quick readings. Upper arm monitors are considered the gold standard due to their accuracy and larger cuff sizes, suitable for stable blood pressure tracking. Finger monitors are less common, offering a highly portable option but generally less accurate than the other types. Each type caters to different user preferences and needs in monitoring blood pressure effectively.</p></p>
