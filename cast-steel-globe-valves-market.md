<p><p>The cast steel globe valves market is segmented based on end connections: Raised-Flange End, Ring-Type Joint End, and Butt-Weld End. Raised-Flange End valves feature a flange that elevates the sealing surface for improved pressure containment, suitable for high-pressure applications. Ring-Type Joint End valves use a metal ring for a secure seal, ideal for continuous operation in demanding environments. Butt-Weld End valves are designed for seamless integration into piping systems, ensuring minimal flow disruption and enhanced mechanical strength.</p></p>
