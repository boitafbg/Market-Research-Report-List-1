<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Thermal Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal camera market is categorized into three main types based on wavelength sensitivity: short-wave infrared (SWIR), mid-wave infrared (MWIR), and long-wave infrared (LWIR) cameras. SWIR cameras detect wavelengths from 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers, ideal for applications like surveillance and quality control. MWIR cameras, sensitive to 3 to 5 micrometers, are used in industrial and military applications. LWIR cameras, operating at 8 to 14 micrometers, are widely utilized for building inspections, firefighting, and medical diagnostics, providing high thermal sensitivity.</p></p>
